Making your own cosmetics

with Marine Lorphelin & Respire

Book this workshop

During this DIY cosmetics workshop, you will learn with former Miss World 2013 Marine Lorphelin to prepare your own beauty products, with organic and simple ressources. All the ingredients are organic products and are provided by our sponsor, RESPIRE. At the end of the session, you will enjoy a friendly moment while testing your creation and discussing with both Marine, and RESPIRE founder, Justine Hutteau. All the recipes are then sent by email so that you can make it again at home.

Marine Lorphelin (born 16 March 1993) is a French model, internal medicine resident and beauty queen who was crowned Miss France 2013 on 8 December 2012, representing the region of Burgundy and then represented France at Miss World 2013 and placed 1st runner up. Her experience in beauty and modelling will be precious to properly consider the impact of cosmetics on our health.

RESPIRE, founded in 2018 by Justine Hutteau, is an eco-friendly and organic cosmetics brand, producing skincare and haircare for both men and women. Efficient, transparent, renewable and made in France. RESPIRE sponsor this workshop to ensure that the product you will make is good for your health.
